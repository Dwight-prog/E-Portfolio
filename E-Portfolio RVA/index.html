Chapter 02

Course Experience

My journey through GEC-ELEC6 — lessons, growth, and discoveries

🌱

Personal Growth

Reading in Visual Arts** allowed me to appreciate art beyond its visual appearance and understand the deeper meanings, emotions, and messages behind every artwork. Throughout the course, I learned how visual elements such as color, composition, symbolism, and perspective influence the way people interpret art and media. The subject challenged me to think critically, analyze creatively, and express my own interpretations with confidence. Through discussions, reflections, and various activities, I became more observant and open-minded, realizing that art is not only about aesthetics but also about culture, identity, communication, and human experience. This course helped me develop a deeper appreciation for creativity and strengthened my ability to connect ideas through visual understanding.

💡

Technical Learnings

This course enhanced my understanding of visual analysis, interpretation, and creative communication through art and media. I learned how to examine different visual elements such as color, texture, symbolism, balance, and composition to better understand the message and meaning behind artworks. The discussions, reflections, and activities improved my critical thinking and observation skills, allowing me to interpret visual content more deeply rather than simply viewing it at surface level. Through analyzing various forms of visual art, I also developed a greater appreciation for creativity, cultural expression, and the role of art in communicating ideas and emotions in society.

⚡

Challenges Encountered

One of the biggest challenges I faced in this course was balancing the subject requirements alongside my other academic responsibilities. Managing my time effectively became difficult, especially when working on reflection papers and activities that required deeper analysis and personal interpretation rather than simple summaries. There were moments when understanding the meaning behind certain artworks and visual materials challenged my creativity and critical thinking skills. However, these experiences pushed me to become more patient, observant, and open-minded. I learned how to express my thoughts more clearly, work under pressure, and overcome creative blocks while still producing meaningful and quality outputs.

🤝

Collaboration Experiences

Working with my classmates during discussions, reflections, and collaborative activities allowed me to appreciate the value of teamwork and the importance of understanding different perspectives. Through sharing ideas and interpretations about artworks, films, and visual materials, I realized that collaboration is not simply about completing tasks together, but also about listening, respecting opinions, and learning from one another’s insights. These experiences helped me improve my communication skills, become more open-minded, and develop patience when working with others. By collaborating with my peers, I gained a deeper understanding of the subject and learned how collective ideas can create more meaningful and thoughtful outputs.

🧠

Problem-Solving Skills

This course helped me develop a more thoughtful and analytical approach to problem-solving through the interpretation and analysis of visual artworks and media. Instead of relying on first impressions, I learned to carefully observe details, identify deeper meanings, and consider different perspectives before forming conclusions. The activities and discussions encouraged me to think critically, express my ideas clearly, and appreciate how visual elements can communicate complex messages and emotions. These experiences strengthened my creativity, observation skills, and ability to analyze situations more effectively, which I can also apply to my academic work, programming projects, and everyday decision-making.

🚀

Future Aspirations

This course inspired me to become someone who not only appreciates art but also understands the value of creativity, culture, and visual expression in everyday life. As I continue my journey in the field of Information Technology, I aspire to combine technical skills with creativity and critical thinking to create meaningful and user-friendly digital experiences. Reading in Visual Arts taught me that visuals have the power to communicate emotions, ideas, and stories that words sometimes cannot express. In the future, I hope to apply this understanding in designing projects and technologies that are not only functional but also engaging, creative, and impactful to people and communities.

Film: Concepts, Philippine Cinema, Film Analysis, & Approaches to Film Criticism:

In this discussion paper, I explored the different concepts and approaches used in understanding and analyzing films, particularly within the context of Philippine cinema. Through various methods of film analysis, I examined how movies communicate deeper meanings through storytelling, symbols, visuals, and sound. The discussion highlights how films are more than just entertainment — they serve as reflections of culture, society, emotions, and human experiences. By studying approaches such as text-based analysis, semiotic analysis, topic-based analysis, and picture and sound analysis, I gained a deeper appreciation for the artistic and critical elements that shape audience interpretation and cinematic expression.

A Mentor Who Made a Difference

From the very first session of GEC-ELEC6, our instructor set a tone that was refreshingly different from what I expected. Instead of dry lectures and passive note-taking, we were challenged to think, to reflect, to engage — and that made all the difference. There was a genuine warmth in the way our instructor approached teaching: always present, always encouraging, and always pushing us to go deeper than the surface.

What I appreciated most was how our instructor created space for every voice in the classroom. No question was too small, no perspective too unconventional. The discussions were dynamic and thought-provoking, and I often left class with my mind buzzing with new ideas. That kind of intellectual stimulation is rare, and I'm grateful to have experienced it.

This course, under this instructor's guidance, has been one of the most meaningful learning experiences of my college journey. Thank you for your patience, your passion, and your unwavering commitment to our growth. The lessons from GEC-ELEC6 — both the technical and the deeply human ones — will stay with me for years to come.
